About Urban Taste

A simple, cozy restaurant in the heart of the city – perfect for family dinners, quick lunches and weekend catch-ups.

We believe good food doesn’t have to be complicated. Our chefs use fresh ingredients, simple recipes and bold flavours to create dishes you’ll want to come back for. From wood-fired pizzas to desi-style mains and classic desserts, our menu has something for everyone.

The ambience is warm, with soft lighting, comfortable seating and music that lets you talk without shouting. Whether you’re on a date, with friends or having a work lunch, Urban Taste makes it feel easy.

  • Vegetarian & non-vegetarian options
  • Separate kids’ menu on weekends
  • Hygienic kitchen & quality ingredients
