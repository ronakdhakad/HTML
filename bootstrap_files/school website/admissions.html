Admissions 2025–26

Simple and transparent admission process for Nursery to Grade XI.

Admission Process

  1. Submit enquiry form online or visit the school office.
  2. Attend counselling session and campus tour.
  3. Fill the registration form and pay the registration fee.
  4. Interaction / assessment (as per grade).
  5. Confirmation of admission and fee payment.

Documents Required

  • Birth certificate (attested copy)
  • Previous school report card (for Grade II and above)
  • Transfer certificate (for Grade II and above)
  • 4 passport size photographs of the child
  • 1 photograph of each parent / guardian
  • Aadhar card copy (where applicable)
