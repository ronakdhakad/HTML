Academics & Curriculum

CBSE-aligned, concept-focused learning from Nursery to Grade XII with strong support systems.

Academic Structure

Our academic program is carefully designed to match the developmental needs of children at each age. The curriculum is aligned with CBSE guidelines, while also integrating life skills, technology and activity-based learning.

Foundational Years (Nursery – Grade II)

Learning through play, stories, music, movement and hands-on activities. We focus on language, early maths, motor skills, social behaviour and emotional development.

Preparatory & Middle Years (Grade III – VIII)

Strong focus on core subjects – English, Hindi, Mathematics, Science, Social Science – with computer education, art, physical education and value education.

  • Concept-based teaching with real-life examples
  • Weekly assessments & regular feedback
  • Olympiads, quizzes and project work
Secondary & Senior Secondary (Grade IX – XII)

Intensive preparation for board exams with a balanced approach. We offer Science, Commerce and Humanities subject combinations with guidance on competitive exams and careers.

  • Regular tests, pre-boards and doubt-clearing sessions
  • Guidance for JEE / NEET / CUET / other entrances through partners
  • Career counselling and profile building support

Assessment & Support

Assessments are designed to measure understanding, application and skills – not just memory. Parents receive regular updates through report cards, parent-teacher meetings and the school app.

Key Features
  • CBSE curriculum with NEP-aligned practices
  • Smart classrooms and digital content
  • Well-equipped science & computer labs
  • Library with 5,000+ books
  • Language labs and communication training
